📝 How to Use

  1. Upload one or more photos using the drag & drop area or click to browse.
  2. Choose Passport Photos or PAN Card, then pick the required size.
  3. Click Open Crop Tool to frame the photo, then click Apply Crop.
  4. Use Adjustments to fine-tune brightness, contrast, or background colour.
  5. Click Generate PDF for a print-ready sheet, or ⬇ Download JPEG for a single file.
  6. 3.5 × 4.5 cm photos are printed landscape on a portrait A4 — 4 per row.
  7. PAN card JPEGs are auto-compressed to meet official size limits (18 KB / 9 KB).
