📝 How to Use

  1. Upload one or more passport photos using the drag & drop area or click to browse.
  2. Select the required photo size from the dropdown.
  3. Click Open Crop to crop the photo to the correct aspect ratio. Use Zoom + / - to adjust, then click Apply.
  4. Use the Adjustments section to fine-tune brightness, contrast, or background colour.
  5. Select the quantity required from the dropdown.
  6. Click Generate PDF — the PDF will download automatically and a record will be saved in History.
  7. Note: For 3.5 × 4.5 cm, photos are printed landscape (4.5 cm wide × 3.5 cm tall) on a portrait A4 PDF — 4 photos per row.
