📋 Instructions

How to Use

  1. Fill in Agreement Details — date and type.
  2. Enter Licensor (Owner) details.
  3. Add one or more Tenants with their details.
  4. Fill the Licensed Premises (property) details.
  5. Set Period & License Fee — end date and amounts in words calculate automatically.
  6. Optionally add Special Conditions.
  7. Upload Passport Photos for all parties.
  8. Click Generate PDF to download the agreement.

⚡ Auto Features

• End date auto-calculates from start date + duration.
• Amount in words fills automatically when you type a number.
• Photos appear in the PDF signature table automatically.

📌 Note

This generator is for use in Pune, Maharashtra only. The PDF matches the exact notarized format under the Maharashtra Rent Control Act, 1999.
